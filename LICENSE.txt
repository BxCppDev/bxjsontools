bxjsontools amalgamates  the jsoncpp  library (by  Baptiste Lepilleur,
https://github.com/open-source-parsers/jsoncpp)   which  is   released
under the MIT License.

bxjsontools reuse large parts of  the JsonSerializer library (by Simon
Gene  Gottlieb, https://github.com/SGSSGene/JsonSerializer/)  which is
released under some kind of Public Licence.

bxjsontools, as the Bayeux library,  is released under the GNU General
Public License (GPL 3.0).

See the' licensing' directory for details.
