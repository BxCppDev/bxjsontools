bxjsontools amalgamates  the jsoncpp  library (by  Baptiste Lepilleur,
https://github.com/open-source-parsers/jsoncpp)   which  is   released
under the MIT License.

bxjsontools reuses large  parts of the JsonSerializer  C++ library (by
Simon   Gene  Gottlieb,   https://github.com/SGSSGene/JsonSerializer/)
which is released under some kind of Public Licence.

bxjsontools, like the Bayeux library,  is released under the GNU General
Public License (GPL 3.0).
